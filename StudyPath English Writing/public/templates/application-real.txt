应用文真题套用模板

1，假设你是李华，你的美国朋友Tom对中国传统文化很感兴趣，请你写一封邮件，向他推荐一本介绍中国文化的书籍。

Dear Tom,

I hope this email finds you well. Knowing that you are deeply interested in traditional Chinese culture, I am writing to recommend a book to you.

The book I want to recommend is titled "A Brief History of Chinese Culture", which provides a comprehensive introduction to various aspects of Chinese culture, including history, philosophy, art, and customs.

This book is particularly suitable for you because it is written in clear and accessible language, making it easy for foreign readers to understand. Additionally, it contains numerous illustrations and examples that help bring the content to life.

I believe this book will not only satisfy your curiosity about Chinese culture but also deepen your understanding of China's rich heritage.

If you have any questions after reading the book, please feel free to ask me. I would be more than happy to discuss it with you.

Best regards,
Li Hua
